Movie Review

Avengers: Infinity War Movie Explanation

  • By Editorial Staff
  • October 10, 2024 - 1 min

The story begins on Thor's ship. Thanos has already collected the Power Stones and now he comes to get the Space Stones. A fierce battle takes place on the ship, in which Loki is killed and the Hulk is sent to Earth to warn others. Thanos's goal is to destroy half of the life in the universe, because according to him, overpopulation will exhaust resources and make life miserable. That is why he wants to collect the six Infinity Stones.

The heroes of the world begin to become active one by one. Tony Stark, Doctor Strange and Spider-Man together face Thanos's army. On the other hand, Captain America, Black Widow and Falcon also come out of hiding and fight. Thanos' main target was the Mind Stone on Vision's forehead, so everyone comes together to protect him.

The Guardians of the Galaxy team tries to stop Thanos, but he takes the Reality Stone. Meanwhile, Thor, Rocket, and Groot team up to create a new weapon, the Stormbreaker, because they knew that only powerful weapons could stop Thanos. On the other hand, Thanos sacrifices Gamora to obtain the Soul Stone, because to get this stone, one must sacrifice someone they love.

Seeing the possibility of the future, Doctor Strange realizes that there is only one way, so he willingly gives the Time Stone to Thanos. Meanwhile, the Avengers start the final battle in Wakanda to save the Mind Stone in Vision's head. But Thanos arrives there and finally takes it. Even though Thor hits Thanos with his new weapon, it is not enough, because Thanos puts the gauntlet on his finger.

In a blink of an eye, half of the life in the universe is destroyed. Spider-Man, Doctor Strange, Black Panther, and many of the Guardians disappear into dust. Only a few heroes survive, such as Tony Stark, Captain America, Thor, Black Widow, and the Hulk. The film's final scene shows Thanos calmly watching the sunrise, while the Avengers are left helpless.
